Briefing

Your participation in this study involves observing a set of virtual characters standing in a line to purchase a copy of a video game. You will observe their actions and how they go about purchasing the item over two runs. Following each scenario, you will be asked to fill out a survey rating the game along several measures.

To start a scenario, you will click on the start button on the top left. Once clicked the button is disabled untill the next run and you have to observe the changes in the characters on the screen as they go from right to left, to the shopkeeper to purchase their copy of the game Destiny.
